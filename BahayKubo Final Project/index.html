About Us

The bahay kubo is a trademark of our history, made out of bamboo and nipa leaves the Bahay Kubo is truly a work of art. We normally see these houses when we visit the province, but some Airbnbs have given the traditional house a modern twist to make them more comfortable and, yes, Instagrammable. For a rustic stay that lets you go back to basics, here are some bahay kubos you can rent nationwide.
